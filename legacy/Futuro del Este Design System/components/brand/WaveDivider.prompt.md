The signature layered wave — use to separate sections or cap a hero, echoing the logo's curves.

```jsx
<WaveDivider gradient height={80} />
<WaveDivider fill="var(--fde-emerald)" flip />
```

Props: `fill` (solid color), `gradient` (brand wave ramp), `height`, `flip` (wave hangs from the top edge). Place at the bottom of a colored hero or between two sections.
